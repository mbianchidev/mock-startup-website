Frequently Asked Questions

Can I start with the Starter plan and upgrade later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade to a higher tier plan at any time. Your existing configuration and data will automatically transfer to your new plan.

What kind of Kubernetes clusters are supported?

We support all CNCF-certified Kubernetes distributions including EKS, AKS, GKE, as well as self-managed clusters like Rancher, OpenShift, and plain vanilla Kubernetes.

How do you handle multi-cloud deployments?

Our Professional and Enterprise plans include tooling for unified management across different cloud providers. This includes normalized infrastructure definitions, cross-cloud networking, and unified observability.

What IaC tools are supported?

We support all major infrastructure as code tools including Terraform, CloudFormation, Pulumi, Ansible, and Chef. Our platform provides a unified interface for managing resources across these tools.

Do you offer custom pricing for startups?

Yes, we have special startup programs for early-stage companies. Please contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs and eligibility.
