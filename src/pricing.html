Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform. Click on a question to see the answer.

Can I start with the Starter plan and upgrade later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade to a higher tier plan at any time. Your existing configuration and data will automatically transfer to your new plan.

What kind of Kubernetes clusters are supported?

We support all CNCF-certified Kubernetes distributions including EKS, AKS, GKE, as well as self-managed clusters like Rancher, OpenShift, and plain vanilla Kubernetes.

How do you handle multi-cloud deployments?

Our Professional and Enterprise plans include tooling for unified management across different cloud providers. This includes normalized infrastructure definitions, cross-cloud networking, and unified observability.

What IaC tools are supported?

We support all major infrastructure as code tools including OpenTofu, CloudFormation, Pulumi, Ansible, and Puppet. Our platform provides a unified interface for managing resources across these tools.

Do you offer custom pricing for startups?

Yes, we have special startup programs for early-stage companies. Please contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs and eligibility. Discount % varies 1:1 based on the % of stocks you give up. Just pay cash and do not bargain.

How does your support work?

Each plan includes different levels of support. Starter plans include business hours support via email and chat. Professional plans add extended hours and priority response times. Enterprise plans include 24/7 support with dedicated technical account managers and guaranteed response times.

Can I try before I buy?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial of any plan with full functionality. No credit card required to start. You can also schedule a personalized demo with our team to see how the platform addresses your specific needs.
